IEP Becca.Lesser Fri, 11/14/2025 - 14:55 The cornerstone of the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) is the entitlement of each eligible child with a disability to a free appropriate public education (FAPE). Under the IDEA, an Individualized Education Program (IEP) is the primary vehicle for providing FAPE. Learn more and find IEP-related guidance, technical assistance, and other resources.
